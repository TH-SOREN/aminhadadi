Amin Haddadi | Poetry and Literature

Amin Haddadi (born September 1992) is a poet, translator, and literary critic based in Tehran. His published works include "Open Wounds and the Melancholy of Tehran" by Afraz Publishing; "Machining the Metal of the Future: Four Parts and an Anthology of Majid Naficy’s Portrait" by Baroo Publishing; and "The Permission to Enter Poem" by Baroo Publishing. His latest poetry book, "Conversation with the Embryo," has been self-published under the imprint "Banners and Stains" in collaboration with Baroo. Several of his articles and translations have appeared in print and online literary journals.

Haddadi is a graduate of cognitive psychology and works in the field of psychotherapy.
